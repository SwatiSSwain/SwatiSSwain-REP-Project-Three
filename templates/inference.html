Inference from our Analysis:

We utilized Random Forest and Logistic Regression modeling to explore a number of economic/societal indicators and their importance in a country’s happiness. By running the Random Forest model we analyzed which combination of indicators gets us to the best fit model, as well as getting a value for the individual indicator’s importance in the model (Feature Importance). We set up our model in the Predict Happiness page as a dynamic model that allows the audience to run the model on their choice of indicators. The Logistic Regression allowed us to explore how well the model could accurately classify a country into our Happiness groups. While this project remained mostly in the exploratory of the indicators/data phase, these models could be further used for predicting a country’s happiness.
